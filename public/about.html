About Me

Hello! My name is Rahul, and I am a passionate software developer with over six years of experience in creating dynamic and user-friendly web applications. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of California, Berkeley, where I graduated with honors. My journey in tech began when I was a teenager, tinkering with HTML and CSS to build my first website. This early interest blossomed into a full-fledged career, and I have since honed my skills in various programming languages, including JavaScript, Python, and Ruby on Rails.

Currently, I work as a Senior Full Stack Developer at a leading tech company in San Francisco, where I lead a team of talented engineers. My role involves designing and implementing robust, scalable web solutions, as well as mentoring junior developers. One of my proudest accomplishments was developing an innovative e-commerce platform that significantly boosted our client's online sales. I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy tackling complex challenges that require creative problem-solving and strategic thinking.

Outside of work, I am an avid traveler and amateur photographer. I love exploring new cultures and capturing the beauty of the world through my lens. In my free time, you can find me hiking the trails of Northern California, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or volunteering at local coding bootcamps to help aspiring developers kickstart their careers. I am always eager to connect with like minded professionals and explore new opportunities to grow and contribute to the tech community.
